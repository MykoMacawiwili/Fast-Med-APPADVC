About Us

Fast-Med a web application that would be able to reserve or have an appointment with a doctor concerning one's own health concerns or issues. Please understand if certain days cannot have an appointment as it is full.


Medical help or Healthcare is one of the most important things we all need right now. Specially with the ongoing virus (Corona Virus). Here in FastMed on of our goal is to provide fast healthcare to everyone in need. With Hospitals being crowded and the pandemic it is not safe to wait outside or inside a hospital with different patients.
